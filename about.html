Topic

In the situation of COVID-19 pandemic, social distancing is the key to stop the spread of the virus. Many people might find it boring to stay at home all day, therefore, they try buying new products as a way to entertain themselves. Many people also take this as opportunity to focus on their health by working out and eating healthier, that is why many of them start to cook their own meals to have better control of their diet. Our passion for food combined with our ability of coding has inspired us to create a website called ICFoodies. This website provides various balanced recipes from different Asian cultures so people can experience food from other countries without travelling while maintaining a healthy diet.

Team Name

Our team's name is Enoki, which is a type of mushroom. We all decided to pick this name as we all enjoy BBQ and hotpot, and Enoki mushroom is a must-have ingredient in both of these dishes. The name is also elegant and evokes an Asian vibe. Furthermore, Enoki means honest in Japanese, and we believe that honesty is one of the most vital traits in a person. As a result, we want to collaborate and offer our product to our users with complete integrity and morality in order to establish a firm foundation of trust in our partnerships. We hope to create a web application for people who appreciate Asian cuisines and wish to spice up their home-cooked meals during the COVID-19 pandemic. This way, not only will people be ensured of the quality of the food, but they will also reduce the risk of getting infected from takeout.

Motivation

Our team members are all from Vietnam where we are raised with Asian cuisine. We value our meals with family as in our culture, family defines our identity and holds a significant importance in our life. Food is the key to erase the generation gap and strengthen the bond within our family. Therefore, we like to cook our own meals rather than buying food from outside.

There are also a lot of benefits of cooking at home according to many food blogger, it is economic, it is a good stress reliever, it can boost your creativity and help with your mental issues. Covid-19 can cause a lot of stress, fear, and anxiety as the number of death and unemployment is rising significantly. Cooking a dish can help distract us from these negative feelings since it is a form of meditate.

Aims

Our goal is to create a food website that gives you a variety of healthy Asian food recipes for you to try out. These recipes can create a balanced diet for those who are concerned about their weight. During the COVID 19 pandemic, many people had to stay at home because of social distancing. Staying indoors all day can have negative impacts on your body so some people had to workout and have a healthy diet. People can’t go outside so they either order food to their home or make the meals themselves. It’s easier to control your diet if you cook yourself and you can find the recipes on our website. We guarantee that the dishes we have on our site are good and healthy options for your diet. We even want to celebrate our culture by suggesting some of the finest Asian dishes. This website is for all of you who are searching for a balanced diet but also want to know more about Asian cuisine.
